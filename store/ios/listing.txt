## Description (4000 char max)
Toroloom is a next-generation trading and investment platform powered by artificial intelligence. Built for Indian traders and investors, Toroloom combines advanced analytics with an intuitive mobile experience.

KEY FEATURES:

📈 F&O Options Chain
- Real-time NIFTY & BANKNIFTY options chain with full Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega, Rho)
- Black-Scholes pricing, implied volatility, and OI analysis
- Max Pain and PCR indicators
- Multi-leg strategy builder with P&L charts

🤖 AI-Powered Insights
- Cognitive trade analysis and personalized insights
- Win/loss ratio, P&L aggregation, sector concentration
- Monthly returns and tax summaries

📊 Portfolio & Analytics
- Real-time portfolio tracking with P&L charts
- Holdings, positions, and trade history
- Advanced analytics: Sharpe ratio, profit factor, broker drag
- Capital gains tax summary (STCG + LTCG)

👥 Social Trading
- Leaderboard of top traders
- Copy trading with allocation management
- Follow traders and track their performance

🔐 Secure & Compliant
- KYC verification (PAN, Aadhaar, DigiLocker, Bank)
- Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
- Two-factor authentication (TOTP)
- Iron Lock — automatic risk management lockdown
- End-to-end encrypted broker session sync

🏦 Broker Integration
- Zero-API Gateway for secure broker connection
- Support for Zerodha, Angel One, Groww
- WebView-based session extraction (no API keys)
- Mock broker for paper trading

📚 Education & Tools
- Interactive learning hub with courses
- Financial calculators (SIP, Lumpsum, EMI, Tax)
- Stock screener with technical indicators
- News feed and market analysis

💳 Subscriptions
- Free, Pro, and Elite tiers
- Razorpay integration for Indian payments
- UPI and card support
