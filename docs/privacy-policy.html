Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Toroloom Technologies ("Toroloom," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our AI-powered trading and investment platform ("the Platform").

We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act), 2023 of India, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and where applicable, the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Platform: Execute trades, display portfolio data, send notifications, deliver educational content.
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: KYC/AML checks, SEBI reporting, audit trail maintenance, tax reporting.
  • Improve the Platform: Analyse usage patterns, fix bugs, optimise performance, train AI models (anonymised data only).
  • Communicate with You: Account notifications, trade confirmations, alerts, support responses, marketing (with your consent).
  • Protect Users and the Platform: Detect fraud, enforce Terms of Service, maintain platform security.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Storage Location: Data is primarily stored on servers located in India. Some data (e.g., crash reports) may be processed on Sentry's servers which may be located outside India.
  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Authentication: We use JWT-based authentication with configurable expiry periods. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls limit internal access to user data.
